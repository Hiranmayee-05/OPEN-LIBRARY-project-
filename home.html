THRILLER a genre where the style of writing evokes a sense of excitement, suspense, and anticipation.

ROMANCE a genre in literature which focuses on the idea of love and relationships of the characters.

SCIENCE-FICTION a genre of speculation, dealing with futuristic concepts like advanced science & tech.

FANTASY a genre involving magical elements, set in a fictional universe inspired by mythology & folklore.

HORROR a genre written with an intention of frightening readers & evoking feelings of terror,shock & disgust.

POETRY a genre that conveys a thought, describes a scene or tells a story in a lyrical arrangement of words.
