THRILLER a genre where the style of writing evokes a sense of excitement, suspense, and anticipation in the reader.

ROMANCE a genre in literature which focuses on the idea of love and relationships of the characters.

SCIENCE-FICTION a genre of speculation, dealing with imaginative & futuristic concepts like advanced science and technology.

FANTASY a genre involving magical elements, typically set in a fictional universe & usually inspired by mythology & folklore.

HORROR a genre that is written with the intention of frightening audiences by evoking feelings of terror, dread, shock & disgust.

POETRY is a type of literature that conveys a thought, describes a scene or tells a story in a concentrated, lyrical arrangement of words.
